## 0. Model and conventions [V]

1D Kitaev chain at the topological point mu=0, Delta=w, Majorana representation
gamma_a (a=0..2L-1), {gamma_a,gamma_b}=2 delta_ab.  Control lambda = alpha,
hopping w, with alpha+w=1.

Measured operator (rate alpha): the distance-3 Bogoliubov bond
    B_j = i gamma_{2j} gamma_{2j+3},     B_j^2 = 1,
projector P_j^pm = (1/2)(1 +/- B_j).  Unraveling: quantum jumps (clicks) with
Born statistics; between clicks the state follows the non-Hermitian no-click
drift generated by h_eff = H - i alpha sum_j (bond term).  Partial post-selection
(PPS): a trajectory with N_clicks clicks is reweighted by zeta^{N_clicks},
zeta in (0,1]; zeta=1 is the bare Born ensemble.

Gaussian description: Majorana covariance Gamma_{ab} = (i/2)<[gamma_a,gamma_b]>,
so for a != b,  <gamma_a gamma_b> = -i Gamma_ab,  and  <B_j>_Q = Gamma_{2j,2j+3}.

## 1. Averaging, Choi doubling, replicas [V/L]

Entanglement and operator-content observables are NONLINEAR in the trajectory
state rho_m (e.g. S = -Tr rho log rho, or <O>^2).  Their Born-rule average
    E[X] = sum_m p_m X(rho_m),   p_m = Tr(K_m rho_0 K_m^dagger),
is computed with R replicas; the constraint sum_m p_m = 1 (probability
normalization) is enforced by taking R -> 1 at the end [Jian-You-Vasseur-Ludwig;
Bao-Choi-Altman].  Each replica is Keldysh/Choi-doubled into a ket (+) and a
bra (-) contour: rho lives in H (x) H*, and a local operator B appears as B_+
(ket) or B_- (bra).

## 2. Where zeta enters: the exact recycling expansion [V]

The dephasing measurement channel for an involutive observable (B^2=1) is, on the
doubled state, generated by the "recycling" map B_+ B_- together with a no-click
counterterm.  PPS weights a click by zeta, so the recycling term carries zeta.
Using P^pm = (1/2)(1 +/- B), the ket-bra projector product is exactly

    P_+ P_- = (1/4) ( 1 + B_+ + B_- + B_+ B_- ).            (2.1)

Collecting the zeta-weighted recycling term against the no-click counterterm, the
measurement part of the doubled generator contains [V, Sec. 6 of CURRENT_THEORY_STATUS]

    single-copy mass  (B_+ + B_-)  with coefficient   (alpha/4)(zeta - 1),     (2.2)
    cross vertex      (B_+ B_-)     with coefficient    (alpha/4) zeta.          (2.3)

KEY: the single-copy coefficient is proportional to (zeta-1); it VANISHES at zeta=1
(Born) and turns on for zeta<1.  zeta enters ONLY through these two terms; this is
exact and structural.
